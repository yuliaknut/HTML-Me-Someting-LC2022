The Rain Frog

Did you know that there are very few animal species who can express judgement as aptly as the rain frog? An average rain frog is extremely beautiful and incredibly well shaped. That is likely the reason they often appear so intimidating to us, humans.

The things we don't know about The Ostrich

The scientific community is not especially vocal about it, but ostriches and rain frogs are clearly related in ways we do not yet fully understand.
